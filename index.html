ABOUT

I'm a developer living in East London and I love solving problems with technology.

From there, I went on to dodge winter for two years and then on to Founders and Coders, a coding bootcamp run by a non-profit organisation in London. I'm currently freelancing for a small digital cooperative on projects with charities - doing what I really love - building apps that look pretty and make people happy.
